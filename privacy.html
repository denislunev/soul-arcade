Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Soul Arcade ("the app") is an entertainment toy. This policy explains how the app handles information.

In plain words: Type a name — your own, a friend's, anything. The letters stay on your device. We don't see them, store them, or send them anywhere. The app is pure math, not magic, and collects zero data.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

How It Works

  • The text you type (letters A-Z) is processed entirely on your device.
  • The text is converted to a numeric hash locally and used to deterministically select a fun verdict from a fixed library of 54 outcomes.
  • The same input always produces the same output — pure deterministic mathematics, no randomness, no AI, no prediction, no mysticism.
  • The text itself is never sent to any server, never stored, and never logged.
  • The app does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any device sensors.
  • The app does not make phone calls, send messages, or read any system data.

Local Storage

The app uses your browser's local storage to remember two things on your device only:

  • The number of scans you have performed (a counter).
  • Which verdicts you have already seen (so the "verdicts remaining to unlock" hint stays accurate).

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can clear it any time by clearing your browser data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Fonts to load a display typeface. When the font is loaded, Google may log standard web request data (IP address, user agent) according to Google's own privacy policy. No data from inside the app is sent to Google.

Sharing Feature

When you tap "Share", your device's system share dialog opens. You choose what to share and with whom. The app does not see or store this action.

The shared link contains a numeric hash (a one-way mathematical fingerprint), not the original text you typed. The hash cannot be reversed to reveal the original input.

Advertising and Tracking

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics, no tracking pixels, no cookies.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences ages 13 and up. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
